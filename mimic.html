Mimic - Web3 Video Streaming

A decentralized video streaming platform where users curated playlists and watched content synchronously

Project Overview

Mimic was a web3 video streaming project where users curated playlists of videos and synchronously watched with other viewers. Users were able to add videos themselves or allow other viewers to add videos.

Sources included direct mp4 links, YouTube, Twitch, and others. Room admins could fully customise their channels.

Key Features

Multi-Source Support

Mimic supported videos from multiple sources including direct mp4 links, YouTube, Twitch, and other platforms.

Synchronous Viewing

Users could watch videos together in real-time, creating a shared viewing experience.

Web3 Integration

Users could tip creators using MetaMask, Kyber Network, Tezos, or by using their debit card through Wyre.
